## Open Project:

**Open Party** is the application of the ['Open Politics'](https://academia.edu/145324339/Open_Politics) research for the practical creation of local political Open Party chapters. It is being developed thgourh years of cooperation in international open government, open governance, and open policymaking collaborative projects.
